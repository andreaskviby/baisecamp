Welcome to Baisecamp - a blog about combining the power of AI Agents with Basecamp, the project management tool I've been using since 2007.

## Why Baisecamp?

The name is a playful blend of "AI" and "Basecamp". After nearly two decades of using Basecamp for project management, I've started integrating AI agents into my workflow. This blog documents that journey.

## What to expect

- Practical tips on using AI agents alongside Basecamp
- Workflow automations and integrations
- Lessons learned from real projects
- The intersection of modern AI tools and timeless project management principles
